Hi, I'm Jacob

Im Jacob the founder and manager of A2D Tiling I am 35 years old was born and raised in Christchurch. I have over 18 years experience in the tiling industry have worked all over New Zealand and Australia. Trade qualified and certified installer/applicator of the industries leading brands. Highly experienced with a wealth of knowledge in all aspects of tiling including substrate preparation, sand/cement screeding, waterproofing, swimming pool/patio, mosaic, large format and natural stone tiling. I have had work featured on grand designs tv show, house of the year magazine along with numerous awards. I have always had a real passion for tiling, from the planning stage meeting and working alongside clients, architects and builders to executing there vision.

I believe it is very important building good relationships based on trust, great communication, good attitude and delivering the highest attention to detail at all times. I love the artistic element to tiling, I enjoy looking at a completed project and admiring the time and skill it takes to achieve the meticulous detailing when striving for perfection. We specialise in all aspects of floor and wall tiling and love a challenge, for us the more detail the better! Architectural and highly detailed tiling is where we have established our great reputation as being one of the leading tile installation companies in Christchurch, we also travel NZ wide for our clients.
